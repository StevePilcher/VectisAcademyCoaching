Our Coaching Ethos

Getting the best out of yourself in endurance sport with our busy lives is a difficult skill to master and often outside help is needed to really get the best out of yourself. At VA we work hard to get the best out of you in the most time efficient way possible.

Everybody has their own individual needs, Goals or targets to achieve, we believe no two people are the same and training must be tailored to the individual. We all respond differently to training methods and what makes a good coach great is being flexible and finding the best plan that suits you and your lifestyle.
